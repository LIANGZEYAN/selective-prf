  The threshold $h{=}0.061$ is the MAD of Qwen-2.5-72B predicted ratios; neutral
  queries (within one MAD of the median) are excluded from evaluation.
  \textbf{Hurt R} and \textbf{Ben R} are recall on PRF-Hurt and PRF-Benefit
  queries respectively; \textbf{Acc} is overall accuracy on non-neutral queries.
  \textbf{AUC} is the area under the ROC curve (Benefit vs.\ Hurt, binary).
  $\boldsymbol{\gamma}$ is Goodman--Kruskal Gamma (ordinal human label vs.\
  continuous predictor score, $n{=}41$).
  $\boldsymbol{\rho}$ is Spearman rank correlation (continuous predictor vs.\
  human $b$-preference ratio, $n{=}41$; $^{*}p{<}0.05$, $^{**}p{<}0.01$).
  The TREC row uses the sign of the nDCG@10 delta (PRF $-$ ColBERT) as the
  binary predictor and its continuous value for AUC/$\gamma$/$\rho$; it serves
  as an \emph{upper bound} requiring ground-truth relevance judgements.
